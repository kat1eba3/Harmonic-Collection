My Daily Commute

Every day, I take the 7:18 bus from Tenafly, NJ to Port Authority. The bus ride takes approximately 50 minutes to an hour. On the bus, I usually listen to music, but in the mornings, I take this time to catch up on sleep!!

Once I reach Port Authority, I walk through 42nd Time Sq station to catch the N,Q,R train downtown. The subway ride is four stops long and honestly goes by really fast. The only bad thing about the subway is the walk through the 42nd Time Sq station, it feels like so much uphill walking!

Once I get to 14th Union Sq I walk five minutes to the UC. In the mornings when I have time to spare, I like grabbing myself a cup of coffee to prepare myself for another day of classes. This is what my daily commute looks like, and I've learned to make the most of it!
